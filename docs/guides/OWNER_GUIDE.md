## Getting Started

### Step 1: Register Your Account

1. Go to PattaMap and click **Login/Register**
2. In the registration form, select **"ğŸ† Establishment Owner"** as your account type
3. Fill in your details:
   - **Pseudonym**: Your display name (min 3 characters)
   - **Email**: Your contact email
   - **Password**: Secure password (min 6 characters)
4. Click **"Create Account"**

âš ï¸ **Important**: Your account requires admin approval before activation. This typically takes 24-48 hours.

### Step 2: Wait for Approval

After registering:
- You'll receive a confirmation message
- An administrator will review your request
- Once approved, you'll receive an email notification
- You can then log in and access your dashboard

### Step 3: Contact Admin for Establishment Assignment

Once your account is approved:
- Contact a PattaMap administrator

- You'll receive confirmation when assignments are complete

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How long does account approval take?**
A: Typically 24-48 hours. Contact support if it takes longer.

**Q: Can I manage multiple establishments?**
A: Yes! Admins can assign multiple venues to your account.

**Q: What if I sell my establishment?**
A: Contact an admin to transfer ownership or remove the assignment.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: No, one account per person. Contact support to merge accounts.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Why can't I edit certain fields?**
A: Your permissions may not include that capability. Contact an admin to request access.

**Q: My changes aren't saving. What should I do?**
A: Check your internet connection, refresh the page, and try again. If the issue persists, contact support.

**Q: Can I delete my establishment?**
A: No, only admins can delete listings. Contact support if you need to remove a venue.

**Q: How do I upload photos?**
A: (v10.1) If you have "Can Edit Photos" permission, click "Edit Establishment" and use the logo upload area in the modal. You can upload a new logo image that will be stored on Cloudinary.

### Permission Questions

**Q: Can I change my own permissions?**
A: No, only administrators can modify permissions.

**Q: Why don't I have employee editing rights?**
A: This permission requires additional verification. Contact an admin to request it.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my permissions?**
A: The system will prevent unauthorized actions and display a friendly error message.
